Components:

  • 30 Trick cards
  • 20 Action cards
  • 1 Leader card
  • 25 Letter tokens

Follow these assembly instructions:

  1. Print all pages containing cards
  2. (Optional) Print all pages containing tokens
    • You can use your own tokens instead of the included letters
  3. Cut, Assemble and Sleeve your cards
  4. Read the rules
  5. Play!

Note that you will also need to add two 6-sided dice (preferably of different colors).

Now you're ready to play! Have fun!
